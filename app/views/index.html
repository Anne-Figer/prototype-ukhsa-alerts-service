Long term planning for cold weather

In anticipation of significant cold periods, year round planning is necessary. Social and healthcare services undertake planning year round to ensure they are prepared for extreme weather events.

The cold weather health watch comprises five levels of response based on cold weather thresholds. The thresholds have been developed to trigger an alert when severe cold weather is likely to significantly affect people's health.

A cold weather health watch system operates in England from the 1 November to 31 March every year, in association with UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA).
